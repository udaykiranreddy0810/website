Welcome

A comfortable stay in Saugus, MA.

If you're looking for a comfortable, affordable place to stay in Saugus, look no further than Chisholm's Motor Inn. Conveniently located on Route 1 North, we offer easy access to local attractions, shopping, dining and more.

Every room is equipped with free WiFi, a 40" flat-screen LED TV with HBO and cable, air-conditioning and heating, and a private bathroom — some units even feature a front patio with chairs. Whether you're here for business or leisure, our cozy, well-maintained rooms are designed for the best rest and relaxation.

We accept all major credit cards and offer free parking and friendly, 24-hour service.

Revere Beach

America's First Public Beach — minutes away

One of the oldest public beaches in the United States, known for its scenic shoreline, ocean views and relaxing atmosphere. The perfect setting for walking, sunbathing, swimming and outdoor recreation — an ideal spot to unwind and enjoy the coastal charm of Massachusetts.
